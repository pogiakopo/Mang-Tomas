About Mang Tomas

Mang Tomas All-Around Sarsa is a popular Filipino sauce known for its distinct, savory flavor. Originally made as a lechon sauce, it has evolved into an all-around condiment that perfectly complements fried, grilled, and roasted dishes.

It is produced by NutriAsia, one of the leading condiment brands in the Philippines.
